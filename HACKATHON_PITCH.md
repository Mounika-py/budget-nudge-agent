# 🚀 Hackathon Round-1 Pitch: FinNudge AI

## 📌 Project Overview
**Name:** FinNudge AI
**Tagline:** Transforming financial habits through AI-powered behavioral intelligence.

---

## 💡 The Problem (The "Why")
Most budgeting apps focus on *tracking* rather than *changing* behavior. Users often look at their overspending after it has happened, leading to "financial regret" but no corrective action. Traditional apps lack personalization and fail to account for the psychological triggers behind spending.

## 🎯 Strong Concept Pitch (The "How")
**FinNudge AI** is a behavioral finance agent that doesn't just show you numbers; it understands *who* you are as a spender. By analyzing real-time transaction data (simulated from platforms like Swiggy and Zomato), it calculates a **Behavioral Risk Score** and identifies your **Spending Personality** (e.g., Impulsive vs. Disciplined).

The core innovation lies in our **AI Nudge Engine**. Instead of generic alerts, FinNudge delivers hyper-personalized, tone-aware "nudges" (Supportive, Strict, or Sarcastic) generated by GPT-3.5 Turbo. These nudges are accompanied by multimedia (memes and sounds) to create a "positive friction" that discourages impulsive spending and encourages better habits through gamification.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Streamlit (For a fast, interactive, and data-rich dashboard)
- **Backend/Analytics:** Python & Pandas (Core logic for risk scoring and transaction processing)
- **AI/LLM:** OpenAI GPT-3.5 Turbo (Personalized behavioral nudge generation)
- **Visualization:** Plotly (Interactive spending breakdowns and risk progress)
- **Authentication:** Simulated OTP Login (Email-based)
- **Multimedia:** Dynamic assets (Memes & Audio) triggered by behavioral thresholds

---

## ✨ Key Features
1.  **🧠 Behavioral Risk Score:** A proprietary formula calculating risk based on category ratios (e.g., food spend) and budget adherence.
2.  **🎭 Personality Detection:** Automatically classifies users into profiles like "Impulsive Spender" or "Disciplined Saver" based on historical patterns.
3.  **🤖 AI Nudge Engine:** Personalized financial advice delivered in different advisor tones, using LLMs to ensure the message resonates with the user's current state.
4.  **🎮 Simulation Mode:** A real-time sandbox where users can add virtual orders to see how their Risk Score and Gamification Level (Gold/Silver/Bronze) react instantly.
5.  **🏅 Gamification:** A reward-based system that upgrades user levels as their behavioral risk decreases, fostering long-term engagement.

---

## 🚀 Future Scalability
- **Real-world Integration:** Connecting to live UPI/Account Aggregator APIs (Setu, Plaid).
- **WhatsApp/SMS Nudges:** Pushing nudges to where the user is, rather than waiting for them to open the app.
- **Predictive ML:** Moving from rule-based personality detection to deep learning models trained on millions of behavioral data points.
- **Collaborative Budgeting:** "Squad" challenges to gamify savings with friends.

---

## 🏁 Conclusion
FinNudge AI moves beyond passive tracking to active behavioral intervention. It’s not just a budget app; it’s a digital financial coach that knows when to be your friend and when to be your drill sergeant.
